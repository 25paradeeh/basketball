Where it came from
  • Basketball was created by Dr. James Naismith in December of 1891 in Springfield, Massachusetts.
  • The point of the game was to throw the ball into the peach basket nailed to the gym balcony.
  • It only had 13 rules
  • The first game was played December 21, 1891
  • First professional team founded in 1898
