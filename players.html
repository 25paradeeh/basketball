Famous Players

Lebron James– Current team is the LA Lakers. Won 2 NBA championship ringsand 1 MVP award. He is a shooting guard and a small forward.

Kobe Bryant– Played for the LA Lakers. Won a 5 time scoring champion with
the Lakers and 2 MVP awards. Altogether he averaged 25.6 points per game.
He passed away January of 2020 in a helicopter with his daughter.
